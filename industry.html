E & TC - Industry Institute Interaction

Interaction between Technical institutions and industry is the need of an hour. This will have great impact on the Engineering Curriculum, exposure of industrial atmosphere to engineering students and subsequent placement of graduating engineers in industries across the globe. To produce proficient graduates ready for the industry, it is necessary to know the requirements of the industries. We at PCCoE have built great rapport with industries in various sectors through Industry Institute Interaction.
Department of Electronics and Telecommunication has received two Centre of Excellence from CoreEL Technologies Pvt. Ltd. Banglore on 13th July 2017 for three years.


  1. VLSI and Embedded Systems: "Xilinx - CoreEL FPGA Center of Excellence"
  2. Advanced VLSI and Embedded Lab: "Mentor Graphics-CoreEL VLSI Center of Excellence"

CoreEL Technologies is having Xilinx Authorized Training Center know as " Sandeepani VLSI & Embedded Training Center.
With this MoU, CoreEL will also help to impart certified training programs for students in weekends, summer holidays; evening classes etc.The students will benefit from this as they will have a course completion certificate, project completion certificate and this will help them in getting a job in the industry.
