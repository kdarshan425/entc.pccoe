Functions Of Board Of Studies


  • 1. To assist in preparing syllabi for various courses keeping in view the objectives of the college, interest of the stakeholders and national requirement for consideration and approval of the Academic Council.
  • 2. Suggest methodologies for innovative teaching and evaluation techniques.
  • 3. Suggest panel of names to the Academic Council for appointment of examiners.
  • 4. Co-ordinate research, teaching, extension and other academic activities in the department/college.
