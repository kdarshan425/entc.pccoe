Electronics and Telecomm. Students Association (ETSA) Objectives of ETSA
• The primary commitment of the ETSA is to provide responsible and equitable student leadership and to mirror the opinions and concerns of all segments of the department.
• The ETSA attempts to motivate students in campus life and form a close-knit interdisciplinary, multinational student community at Electronics and Telecommunication Department.
• The purpose of the ETSA shall be the establishment and maintenance of student government, the enactment and enforcement of the just laws and the promotion of such activities as are appropriate for the student body.
• To promote the general welfare of the students; to encourage personal responsibility, loyalty, and a high sense of honor.

Name of the event: “GEN NEX INDUSTRY” SEMINAR ON INDUSTRY STANDARD 4.0
Brief information of event: Department of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering, had organized “GEN NEX INDUSTRY” One day seminar on INDUSTRY STANDARD 4.0, in association with IETE-PCCOE student forum and IEEE student chapter, Pune which was scheduled on 08th September 2018 at PIMPRI CHINCHWAD COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING(PCCOE),Pune. “GEN NEX INDUSTRY” seminar on INDUSTRY STANDARD 4.0 aimed to provide an opportunity for students as well as professionals to enhance their skills and knowledge about Industry 4.0 standard and give them glimpses of Next-Generation Industry. Around 300 participants including Students , Faculty members and Professionals were benefitted from the Seminar.
