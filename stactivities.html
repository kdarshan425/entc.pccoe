ETSA- ISTE Cell (Electronics And Telecomm. Students Association)


Objectives

  1. The primary commitment of the ETSA is to provide responsible and equitable student leadership and to mirror the opinions and concerns of all segments of the department.
  2. The ETSA attempts to motivate students in campus life and form a close-knit interdisciplinary, multinational student community at Electronics and Telecommunication Department.
  3. The purpose of the ETSA shall be the establishment and maintenance of student government, the enactment and enforcement of the just laws and the promotion of such activities as are appropriate for the student body.
